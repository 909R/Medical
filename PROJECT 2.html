Lilac City Dental is located in Spokane, Washington. Our dental practice offers the
latest in dental technology and provides a friendly, relaxed atmosphere. Dr. Robert
Renck is an experienced dentist and our staff is highly trained. We care for patients
with regular checkups and cleanings, and perform in-depth procedures such as teeth
pulling, teeth straightening, surgery, crowns, fillings, caps, root canals, wisdom teeth
removal, whitening, dental implants, and more. We pride ourselves on excellent
customer service at all times.
